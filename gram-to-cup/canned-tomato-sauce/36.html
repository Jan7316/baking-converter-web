360g Canned Tomato Sauce in Cups

Question

What is 360g of Canned Tomato Sauce in Cups?

Answer

360g of Canned Tomato Sauce is the same as 1 1/4 cups, 3 tbsp, 10.53 tsp (1.47 cups)

How do we know this?

We use density data from the U. S. Department of Agriculture to convert between weights and volumes for a wide range of ingredients.

Cups, Tbsp, Tsp

A U. S. Cup has a volume of 236 ml. A standard tablespoon (tbsp) holds 1/16 of a cup, or 15 ml. Finally, a standard teaspoon (tsp) is 1/3 of a tablespoon, 1/48 of a cup, and holds 5 ml.
